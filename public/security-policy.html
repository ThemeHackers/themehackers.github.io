Security Policy

Last updated: July 2025

1. Introduction

At TH - Dashsecurity, we are committed to protecting the security and privacy of our users. This Security Policy outlines our approach to safeguarding your data and maintaining a secure environment for all users of our platform.

2. Responsible Disclosure

If you believe you have discovered a security vulnerability in our website or services, please report it to us promptly at tigerzaza5678@gmail.com. We appreciate your efforts to help us improve our security.

3. Data Protection

  • All user data is stored securely and access is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • We use industry-standard encryption for data in transit and at rest.
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments are conducted.

4. User Responsibilities

  • Keep your login credentials confidential and do not share them with others.
  • Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) where available.
  • Report any suspicious activity to our security team immediately.

5. Incident Response

In the event of a security incident, we will promptly investigate, mitigate the impact, and notify affected users as appropriate.

6. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Please review it periodically for any changes.

7. Contact

For any security-related questions or concerns, contact us at tigerzaza5678@gmail.com.
